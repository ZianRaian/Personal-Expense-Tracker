Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account data — your name and email address, provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually.
  • Usage data — basic server logs such as request timestamps and IP addresses, retained for security purposes.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data from your device beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To display your expense history and summaries within the app.
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To diagnose technical issues and maintain service reliability.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any automated decision-making.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on servers located in India. We take reasonable technical precautions — including encrypted connections and access controls — to protect your information from unauthorised access.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, all personal data associated with your account is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts — for typography. Google may log font requests per their own privacy policy.

We do not integrate analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media trackers. No expense data is ever shared with third parties.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request deletion of your data, please contact us at support@spendly.app. We will respond within 5 business days.
